What is Scrappy?

Scrappy is a website that allows users to create and share their digital scrapbooking projects. With its user-friendly interface and easy-to-use tools, Scrappy makes it simple for anyone to create beautiful, personalized scrapbooks to treasure their memories.

Variety of themes and templates

Scrappy offers a variety of themes and templates to choose from, allowing users to customize the look and feel of their scrapbooks to match their personal style.

Community aspect

Scrappy has a community aspect where users can share their scrapbooks with friends and family and discover inspiration from other users' projects.

Interactive features

Scrappy offers a variety of interactive features, such as the ability to add videos and audio recordings to scrapbook pages, making it easy to capture the full story of a memory.

Safe and Secure storage

Scrappy offers a safe and secure way to store your memories, all of your Scrapbooks are saved in the cloud, so you can access them from anywhere, and you can also export your scrapbook as PDF, PNG or JPG.
